Tixpy App Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-19

This privacy policy applies to Tixpy applications (each, an “App”) published by Tixpy. Specific apps may have additional notices or in-app disclosures.

Data we collect

  • Account data (if the App offers sign-in): email and basic profile information.
  • App data you enter into the App (records, notes, settings).
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and performance data to improve reliability.

How we use data

  • Provide the App’s core functionality.
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • Improve performance and fix bugs.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers used to operate the App (hosting, analytics, crash reporting) under appropriate agreements.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the App’s operation and for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security, compliance).

Account deletion

If the App supports accounts, you can request account deletion by emailing support@tixpy.com with the subject Delete my account.
