DVD Valet — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

DVD Valet respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Local Library Data: UPCs you scan and any library metadata you add (stored on your device).
  • Camera Access (Optional): Used only to scan UPC barcodes. You can deny permission and still use non-scan features.
  • Diagnostic Information: Basic error logs used to maintain reliability. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (scan UPCs, manage your queued library).
  • Improve reliability and support (debug issues, fix bugs).

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with service providers who help us operate the service (for example, hosting), under appropriate safeguards.

Security

DVD Valet stores your library queue locally on your device. If future versions sync data to a server, network communication will use HTTPS/TLS.

Retention

Local library data remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear data. Diagnostic logs are retained only as needed to operate the service.

Childrens Privacy

DVD Valet is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Camera Permission: You can deny or revoke camera access in device settings; the app remains functional.
